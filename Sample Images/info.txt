There are 4 channels in each image.
Ch1= DIC (bright field)
Ch2= CD34  (channel to test whether there is singal, ie postive cells)
Ch3= PP
Ch4= Nucleus  (use for detecting al lthe cells)

Stack images has 4 channels and 5 slices/channel in 2-4, Ch1 has only one slice
Projections: 4 channels, 1 slice/channel (5 slices maximum projected to one slice)

Cell detection file  (overlay): example of cells detected based on the nuclear channel, and overlaid on the DIC channel
